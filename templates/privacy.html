Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use Spendly, we collect the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We do not collect payment information or government-issued identification.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalising your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to render typefaces. Beyond this, we do not share your data with third-party services, analytics providers, or advertisers. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 5 business days.
